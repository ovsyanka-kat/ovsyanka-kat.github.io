Welcome to Yoga with Kateryna

I believe in the transformative power of yoga to bring balance, strength, and inner peace to your life. Whether you're new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, my classes are designed to support your journey towards a healthier, more mindful lifestyle.

About Me

My name is Kateryna, I'm a certified yoga instructor with [X] years of experience in teaching and practicing yoga. My passion for yoga began in 2012 as a personal practice. It opened a whole new world to me, that led me to study meditation, discover my spirituality, and dive into holism.

Yoga means unity: to unite yourselves in a universal spirit. Yoga empowers you to reconnect with yourself and with nature, to raise your awareness and to live in the present moment.

Extremely grateful for this discovery, it inspired me to share this powerful gift with others. Following this instinct, I studied and became a Yoga instructor so I could give in return what had been given to me.

Through my teachings, I aim to create a safe and welcoming space for everyone, where you can explore and deepen your practice.
